# XStack Scope

## Boundary

XStack is Dominium-local and strict. It is a Dominium governance and proof profile above AIDE, not a generic AIDE product layer.

Q07 records only the AIDE-side bridge boundary. It does not implement XStack proof execution, Dominium Runtime, product behavior, provider integrations, or external repo mutation.

## Portable AIDE Below XStack

Portable AIDE provides:

- declarative Profile/Contract records;
- Harness structural validation and reports;
- generated-artifact source-of-truth and marker rules;
- Compatibility version, migration, replay, upgrade-gate, and deprecation posture;
- queue and evidence discipline.

## Dominium-Local Above AIDE

Dominium/XStack may require:

- stricter review gates;
- proof evidence before adoption;
- explicit compatibility pins;
- generated target review before writing real outputs;
- audit evidence for policy or pin changes.

Those stricter rules belong to Dominium and remain local to the bridge.

## Candidate Future Abstractions

Only after separate review may any XStack lesson become a portable AIDE abstraction. Q07 does not perform that promotion.
