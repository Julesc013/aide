# Q41 Remaining Risks

- Q41 is advisory and no-execution only; it does not perform real target tool
  absorption.
- Tool detection and capability mapping are deterministic heuristics, not
  semantic proof.
- Unknown tools remain preserved and review-required.
- Wrapper plans are not active wrappers and cannot execute old tools.
- No migration, renaming, deletion, or retirement is authorized by Q41.
- Target repositories must generate their own tool inventories, classifications,
  and wrap plans.
- `core/gateway` unittest discovery timed out during validation and remains a
  residual validation gap.
- Q42 is required before move, salvage, or path-alias planning can support any
  future structural tool absorption work.
