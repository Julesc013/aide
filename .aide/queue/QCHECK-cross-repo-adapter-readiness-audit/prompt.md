Checkpoint identity: `QCHECK-cross-repo-adapter-readiness-audit`.

Q21, Q24, and the now-available read-only Q22/Q23 target-pilot evidence in
Eureka and Dominium.

preserving AIDE's binding product constraint:

work, model calls, provider calls, network calls, Eureka mutations, Dominium
