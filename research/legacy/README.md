# Legacy Candidate Research

This directory tracks researched candidate IDE families that may matter to AIDE later but are not yet committed host lanes in the repository.

The files here are for curation, prioritization, and unresolved-items tracking. They do not promote a candidate family into `hosts/`, and they do not imply implementation work has started.

Machine-readable candidate records live in `inventory/legacy-ide-families.yaml`. Committed host families remain in `inventory/ide-families.yaml` and `hosts/`.
