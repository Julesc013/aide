# ADR-0006: XStack Remains Dominium-Local

## Status

accepted

## Context

XStack is tied to Dominium's strict local governance and proof posture. Treating it as a generic AIDE product layer would overstate current scope.

## Decision

Keep XStack Dominium-local. Dominium Bridge is the first planned AIDE Bridge target, and XStack remains its strict local governance and proof profile.

## Consequences

- XStack references must stay scoped to Dominium Bridge.
- Q01 does not implement Dominium Bridge or XStack.
- Broader AIDE Bridges require their own queue items and evidence.

## Supersedes / Superseded By

Supersedes: none. Superseded by: none.
